Love For The Game

I played basketball here and there during 5th grade but I finally started to take it more seriously when I joined the basketball team at my elementary school in the 6th grade. Basketball was fairly new to me at the time and I started playing on the team as a bench warmer during 6th grade. By 7th grade I improved enough to be a shooting guard and a back-up pointguard for my team, and by 8th grade I was finally the starting pointguard for my school. Even though I have only been playing the sport competitively for 4 years I have developed a great love for it, and I want to continue playing the sport throughout my life, especially in highschool.

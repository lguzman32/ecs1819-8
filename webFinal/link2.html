What Will It Be

I don't really know what my legacy will be but as I grow and mature im starting to see the world through a bigger keyhole and I start to think to myself what my legacy will be. I want to leave a great impact in this generation, I want to do something that will be remembered for hundreds of years or something that will be remembered in the lives of some individuals forever. I want to make the world a better place than it is today so that future generations could prosper and live comfortably.
